Documenting the challenge
& the potential of TES
Industrial heat is challenging to decarbonize due to the scale of technology & infrastructure required to meet the demand for heat without fossil fuels. More often than not, implementation of technologies comes down to cost competitiveness and awareness of technologies.
Our knowledge base documents the current cost competitiveness of different energy sources. Additionally, it provides an overview of how TES technologies could enable the transition into a cleaner future.
Explore

Informing policy planning
essential for electrification
Without political support, wind and solar technologies would not be at the low price they are today. Policy is essential in creating the conditions for clean industrial heat. We built a tool that allows policy makers and stakeholders in the industry to imagine how these decisions could influence heat economics.
Our exploratory tool allows users to visualize the impact of carbon taxes on the decarbonization of heat across the United States. Users can imagine different scenarios and explore outcomes.
