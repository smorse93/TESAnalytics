Vision
“Deliver clean and cheap renewable heat to enable industrial decarbonization”
Mission
We believe that Thermal Energy Storage (TES) will play a critical role in creating a greener industrial sector. Given the technology’s nascency, significant enablement is required. Our analytics-driven approach helps us deliver exceptional value to the sector to spearhead growth.
Strategy
“To enable TES adoption, we raise awareness about the technology, support policy making and implement projects. Data is the core of our business, allowing us to make smarter decisions about TES investments so that we can deliver cleaner & cheaper heat. ”
TES Analytics is an effort to enable industrial heat storage and decarbonization
